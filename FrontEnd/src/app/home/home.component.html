About us


Stagek is a website that makes finding internships and interns as fluid as ever in Tunisia. Accessible to all, it brings together on a single innovative and efficient media, on the one hand, students and young graduates, and on the other hand, companies and recruitment professionals ... This multicriteria site has been thought out, designed and presented with the sole aim of optimizing your research whether you are candidates or recruiters. A multitude of tips, tricks, and useful information will allow you to better manage your request. The innovations offered by Stagek undoubtedly lie in its advanced functionalities. Particular attention is paid to supporting Internet users: simplicity, time savings, ease of use, exclusive services, etc. By optimizing their search in Stagek, the candidate can then focus on the quality of their applications, on writing and updating their CV. Stagek offers companies and recruiters high-level services allowing them to better manage the recruitment process and to succeed in their HR strategies.
